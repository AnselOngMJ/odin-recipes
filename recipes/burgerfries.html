Burger & Fries

Description

Who doesn't love making their own burger and fries? If you're good, it's better than fast food!

Ingredients

  • Burger buns
  • Meat patty
  • Bacon
  • Lettuce
  • Cheese slices
  • Potatoes

Steps

  1. Wash and peel the potatoes.
  2. Cut the potatoes into slices.
  3. Soak the potatoes in cold water and pat dry.
  4. Deep fry the potatoes and toss with salt.
  5. Toast the burger buns.
  6. Grill the meat patty and cook the bacon.
  7. Assemble the burger and melt the cheese with a blowtorch.
  8. Serve with fries on a plate and your favourite sauce.
