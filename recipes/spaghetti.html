Spaghetti with Meatballs

Description

It's pretty simple, not much to say about it. I bought frozen meatballs but you can make them yourself if you have the time to.

Ingredients

  • 75 g spaghetti
  • 7 frozen swedish meatballs
  • 3 cloves garlic, diced
  • 100 g cherry tomatoes
  • ½ teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Steps

  1. Boil water in a pot and salt it well.
  2. Add your spaghetti and cook to al dente.
  3. Microwave the frozen meatballs following package instructions.
  4. Heat olive oil in a pan and add garlic and red pepper flakes.
  5. Add your spaghetti and meatballs and just toss them around.
  6. Serve immediately!
