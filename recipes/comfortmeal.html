My Comfort Meal

Description

This meal isn't the healthiest but I love eating this. Not much effort is required but the luncheon meat and mushrooms go well with plain white rice.

Ingredients

  • 0.5 cup uncooked white rice
  • 200 g luncheon meat
  • 100 g button mushrooms
  • 1 cup frozen mixed vegetables
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon cooking oil

Steps

  1. Wash the rice and cook in a rice cooker.
  2. While waiting for the rice to cook, cut the luncheon meat and mushrooms into slices.
  3. Add oil to a pan and place the luncheon meat.
  4. Cook and flip both sides until crispy.
  5. Let them rest on another plate and add your mushrooms.
  6. Add salt and black pepper when the mushroom turns brown.
  7. Serve on top of steaming white rice!
