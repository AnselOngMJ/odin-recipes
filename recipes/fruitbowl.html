Fruit Bowl

Description

Just toss some fruits in the blender then toss some fruits on top and you've got a meal!

Ingredients

  • Blackberries
  • Raspberries
  • Strawberries
  • Mangoes
  • Almonds

Steps

  1. Toss some frozen fruits in a blender and blend until smooth.
  2. Struggle to get all of it out into a bowl and add some fruits on top.
  3. Optionally, add more fruits onto your table so you can take a nice picture.
  4. Take the fruit bowl for yourself and let someone else eat the remaining fruits on the table.
