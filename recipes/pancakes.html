Pancakes

Description

There can never be enough pancakes! Keep stacking them until someone screams at you to stop!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Baking powder
  • Sugar
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Mix some ingredients and turn it into pancake batter.
  2. Add the batter to a pan and flip when one side is cooked.
  3. Repeat the steps to make another pancake.
  4. Repeat the previous steps to make more pancakes.
  5. Double the number of pancakes.
  6. Stack them from smallest to biggest on a plate with the smallest at the bottom.
  7. Grab 2 more plates and play the Tower of Hanoi!
  8. When you succeed, reward yourself by consuming the pancakes with some toppings.
